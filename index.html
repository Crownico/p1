Mapping Women's Contributions in Malaysian Academic History

A Digital Archive Prototype

Overview

This project aims to recognize and digitally preserve the legacy of women who have played vital roles in shaping higher education in Malaysia. By developing a small-scale digital archive prototype, the project will document and highlight the achievements of notable female academics and institutional leaders.

The archive will feature biographical entries, career milestones, institutional affiliations, and multimedia content such as images, document scans, and interactive elements. This digital initiative helps enhance public accessibility and promote greater awareness of the underrepresented contributions of women in Malaysian academia.

Research Questions

1

How can digital archives be used to highlight the role of women in the development of Malaysian higher education?

2

What tools and metadata best support the visibility and accessibility of historical academic contributions by women?

Objectives

1

To develop a functional prototype of a digital archive that documents influential female figures in Malaysian universities.

2

To apply visual storytelling and digital humanities tools to present biographical narratives, career milestones, and institutional contributions.

3

To assess best practices in metadata creation, accessibility, and user interface design for historical digital archives.

4

To promote inclusivity and diversity by recognizing the contributions of women in a traditionally male-dominated academic space.
